About Me

Hello! I am Stephien, a first-year Computer Science student at Lincoln University with a focus on software engineering. My journey into the world of technology was influenced by my passion for creativity and a drive to build innovative solutions. I find joy in coming up with new ideas and bringing them to life. This creative mindset was what led me to explore the field of software engineering.

At Lincoln University, I am immersing myself in the fundamentals of computer science while honing my skills in programming, system design, and software development. Each day, I am inspired by the potential to contribute to groundbreaking projects and develop solutions that can make a meaningful impact on the world. I am eager to apply my growing knowledge and skills in a practical setting.

My long-term goal is to become a successful full-stack software engineer who makes a significant impact in the tech industry on a global scale. I aspire to develop innovative solutions that address complex challenges and drive technological advancements. To achieve this, I am actively seeking internship opportunities where I can gain hands-on experience, collaborate with industry professionals, and further develop my technical expertise. If you’re looking for a passionate and dedicated intern who is eager to contribute and learn, I would love to connect.

Outside of my career, I am a lady who is marvelously helped by God. I move forward in life guided by the promise of Jeremiah 29:11: “For I know the plans I have for you,” declares the Lord, “plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.” I am profoundly grateful to God for the journey I am on, recognizing that my accomplishments and opportunities are blessings I do not take for granted. My life philosophy centers on my belief that every challenge is an opportunity for growth and that by embracing resilience and continuous learning, I can make a positive impact on the world. I am a friendly and welcoming individual who values putting others' interests ahead of my own. I enjoy engaging in meaningful and productive discussions and find inspiration in motivational books that encourage personal growth and resilience.
